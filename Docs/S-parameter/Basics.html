S-/Z-/Y-/T-parameter conversion

1 S to Z, Z to S

An N-port network is assumed. Its terminal voltages and terminal sink currents are defined as ii and ii. The impedance matrix Z is given and relates vi and ii as [v0v1⋮vN−1]=Z[i0i1⋮iN−1].

Considering S-parameter, the voltages are normalized by the square-roots of port impedances as v0=zP0u0,v1=zP1u1,⋯vN−1=zP(N−1)uN−1. Representing in matrix-vector notation, V=[zP00⋯00zP1⋯0⋮⋮⋱000⋯zP(N−1)]U=SZPU. and the currents are normalized by the square roots of port admittances as i0=yP0h0,i1=yP1h1,⋯iN−1=yP(N−1)hN−1. Representing in matrix-vector notation, I=[yP00⋯00yP1⋯0⋮⋮⋱000⋯yP(N−1)]H=SYPH. Including (3) and (5) into (1), it is modified as SZPU=ZSYPH

U=SZP−1ZSYPH=SYPZSYPH

Decomposing the normalized voltages and the normalized currents into their incident and reflected components, U=UI+UR=(I+S)UI,H=HI−HR=UI−UR=(I−S)UI.

Including (8),(9) into (7), (I+S)UI=SYPZSYP(I−S)UI(I+S)(I−S)−1=SYPZSYP

Z=SZP(I+S)(I−S)−1SZP

(I+S)=SYPZSYP(I−S)(I+SYPZSYP)S=(SYPZSYP−I)S=(SYPZSYP+I)−1(SYPZSYP−I)

2 S to Y, Y to S

Y=Z−1=(SZP(I+S)(I−S)−1SZP)−1Y=SYP(I−S)(I+S)−1SYP

(I−S)(I+S)−1=SZPYSZP

(I−S)=SZPYSZP(I+S)

(I−SZPYSZP)=(SZPYSZP+I)S

S=(I+SZPYSZP)−1(I−SZPYSZP)

3 S to T, T to S

A transport matrix can be applied only to an 2N-port network. port[0] to port[N-1] are on the left side of the network. port[N] to port[2N-1] are on the right side of the network. [BUBL]=[TULTURTLLTLR][AUAL]

Scattering matrix is applied to the 2N-port network, [BUAL]=[SULSURSLLSLR][BLAU]

Decomposing (22), BU=TULAU+TURAL,BL=TLLAU+TLRAL.

Decomposing (23), BU=SULBL+SURAU,AL=SLLBL+SLRAU.

3.1 S to T conversion

Moving BL to LHS and moving AL to RHS in (27), BL=SLL−1(AL−SLRAU)

Comparing (28) and (25), TLL=−SLL−1SLR,TLR=SLL−1. Replaceing BL in (26) with LSH in (28),

BU=SULSLL−1(AL−SLRAU)+SURAU

BU=(SUR−SULSLL−1SLR)AU+SULSLL−1AL Comparing (32) and (24),

TUL=SUR−SULSLL−1SLR=SUR+SULTLL,

TUR=SULSLL−1=SULTLR

3.2 T to S conversion

Moving BL in (25) to RHS and moving AL in (25) to LHS, AL=TLR−1(BL−TLLAU). Comparing (35) and (27), SLL=TLR−1,SLR=−TLR−1TLL. Replacing AL in (24) with (35), BU=TULAU+TURTLR−1(BL−TLLAU),BU=TURTLR−1BL+(TUL−TURTLR−1TLL)AU Comparing (39) and (26), SUL=TURTLR−1,SUR=TUL−TURTLR−1TLL
